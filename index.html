Play the game of Pig Dice!

Each turn you and your opponent will repeatedly roll a dice until either a 1 or a player decides to "hold". If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn. If the player rolls any other number, it is added to their turn total and the player's turn continues. If a player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to their total score, and it becomes the next player's turn. The first player to score 100 or more points wins.
